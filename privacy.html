Privacy Policy

This app automates video uploads to TikTok using n8n. Here’s how we handle your data:

  • We collect your TikTok access token and user ID to authenticate and post videos.
  • We do not store your data long-term; tokens are used only during the upload process.
  • We do not share your data with third parties, except as required by TikTok’s API.
  • You can revoke access at any time via your TikTok account settings.
